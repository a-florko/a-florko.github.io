PROJECT AUTHORS

Larisa Mozgova
Director of the Zhytomyr Regional Library for Children and Youth, member of the National Union of Local Historians of Ukraine, member of the Ukrainian Library Association.
Lyudmila Zalishchuk
Journalist of the independent site "Zhytomyr Shchodnya", member of NUJU, AUT "Prosvita" named after T. Shevchenko, Coordination Council on the affirmation of Ukrainian national and civic identity of Zhytomyr RMA, deputy head of NGO "LA DA". From 2021-2025 worked in the museum sphere, specifically joined the development of the "Read Zhytomyr" project.
Svitlana Hres
Literary pseudonym Svitlana Shtatska. Head of the Zhytomyr Regional Organization of the National Union of Writers of Ukraine, writer, public figure, laureate of many literary contests and festivals, holder of the Order of Princess Olga III class. While working at the Zhytomyr Regional Literary Museum, worked on creating the "Read Zhytomyr" project.
Yevgeniya Kanchura
Head of the Department of Theoretical and Applied Linguistics at Zhytomyr Polytechnic State University, co-chair of the Center for Fantasy Literature Research at the T.H. Shevchenko Institute of Literature of the National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine, translator.

Information about authors, works, and locations is based on archival materials of the Literary Department of the Zhytomyr Regional Museum of Local Lore. Members of the scientific circle "Intertextual Studies" of the Department of Theoretical and Applied Linguistics of Zhytomyr Polytechnic State University participated in the preparation of materials.

Illustrative photos are taken from the public domain, social networks (in particular, Andrii Morozov's personal blog), as well as works added by circle members.
